About Wickersley Old Village Cricket Club

We're a Village cricket club offering a variety of cricket from ECB Premier League Cricket, Women's Cricket & Junior Cricket including Dynamos and All Stars. We have 3 senior teams playing in the Yorkshire Cricket Southern Premier League in the Championship, Division 1 & Division 5.

Our Women's section - The Vixens, have 3 teams in the Yorkshire Cricket Premier League competing in Division 1 for Hardball and Softball, and also in Division 3 Softball.

We have junior teams in the Ben Jessop Junior Cricket League: U9s, U11s, U13s, U15s, U18s plus our Junior Vixens. Our successful All Stars programme will be back again this year along with Dynamos cricket.

Join WOVCC for just £15/year and enjoy drink discounts, member benefits, and access to exclusive content. Thank you to all our members, guests and sponsors for making each season a great success.
