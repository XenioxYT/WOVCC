Instructions

  1. Toggle Live Section: Turn on to show the live match section on the homepage. Turn off to show regular fixtures/results.
  2. Select Match: Choose ONE match for the livestream. All of today's matches will automatically show their live scores via Play-Cricket widgets.
  3. External Livestream (Optional): Add a YouTube or other video stream URL for the selected match. The livestream will display the match name above the video.
  4. Save: Click "Save Configuration" to apply your changes.
  5. Clear: Use "Clear All" to reset the configuration and turn off the live section.
